About Lisa K. Shafii, Esq.

Special Education Attorney | Founder, LKS Law

Lisa K. Shafii is a special education attorney who brings legal insight, personal experience, and practical strategy to families navigating complex school district processes. A graduate of Loyola Law School and a member of the California Bar since 2013, Lisa began her legal career in plaintiff-side civil litigation, where she developed strong skills in advocacy and negotiated resolution. Since 2016, she has focused her practice exclusively on special education law.

As the mother of two children with special needs, Lisa understands firsthand the emotional and logistical challenges families face when seeking appropriate educational services. Her dual perspective — as both a parent and an attorney — allows her to guide clients with clarity, compassion, and purpose.

Lisa’s background includes roles at Bet Tzedek, Public Counsel, and the Alliance for Children’s Rights, along with service as a law clerk for the Los Angeles District Attorney’s Office. She holds a B.A. in Mass Communication and Journalism from Towson University and has lived in Los Angeles since 2007.

At LKS Law, Lisa supports families in IEP advocacy, school district negotiations, and due process hearings — always with the goal of ensuring students receive the services and supports they deserve. She is known for her grounded, strategic approach, and her ability to distill complex issues into clear, empowering next steps.

In addition to her private practice, Lisa works in collaboration with HOPE4Families, providing legal services through their special education law team.
